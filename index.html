About the workshop

Having success on social media can feel like a huge, confusing slog. You know that you need to do more than just post and hope…but what exactly do you need to do? Is it really possible to build a brand and win customers on social? It is…but you need a strategy. In this session, you’ll get that strategy.

Social media use has skyrocketed during the coronavirus pandemic. That means there are more potential customers out there for you, but also more competition, as businesses are taking advantage of the opportunity. Success on social media relies on knowing where your audience is, understanding each platform that you use, and targeting your approach so you can reach the people who’ll want to buy from you.
Book your place now to develop your social strategy, grow your brand and win more customers.

Who for? Anyone who wants to use social media to grow their brand, including business owners, marketing managers and social media managers.

In this session, you’ll learn:

  • How people consume content on each social channel (they don’t all work in the same way).
  • How to create a social content strategy that fits your brand values.
  • How to use advertising to grow your brand faster.
  • How to monitor and improve your campaign performance.
  • We’ll share real client data from our own campaigns, so you can see exactly how this works in practice.
